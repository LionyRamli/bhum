Vision

“Tzu Chi” literally means “Giving with Love”. Tzu Chi's mission is to provide material assistance while cultivating love and a sense of humanity in the donor and recipient of the assistance.

Mission

Tzu Chi's dream is to purify the human heart, create a safe and secure society, and prevent the world from disaster. This ideal can only be achieved by cultivating love within everyone. This is what Tzu Chi does by carrying out 4 main missions, 8 traces of Dharma, namely:

1. Charity mission
2. Health mission
3. Educational mission
4. Humanitarian cultural mission
5. International disaster relief
6. Bone marrow donors
7. Environmental preservation
8. Community volunteers
